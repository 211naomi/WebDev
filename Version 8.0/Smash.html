Smash Ultimate

Super Smash Brothers Ultimate came out last year, and I can't put it down. The game is a direct upgrade from the last installment and continues to prove that Nintendo knows what they're doing with this game series. It's selling point is "Everyone is Here!" and certainly delivers on that, bringing back nearly every character from the older games, while also introducing a wide variety of new characters.

Favorite Characters

Bayonetta, The Umbra Witch

Mr Game and Watch, The Classic
