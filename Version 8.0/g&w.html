Mr Game and Watch

Mr Game and Watch doesn't represent a single game, but an entire era of games called "Game and Watch Games" his moveset is heavily inspired by a multitude of these games, which is why Mr Game and Watch is a contender for one of the silliest characters in Smash

Nothin but 9's

Click the phrase above to learn more about Game and Watch's Moves

The bread and butter of Game and Watch's kit is his Judge Attack, he swings a hammer and displays a number that is one through nine. Each of these numbers have varying effects, a 7 for example will do moderate damage and also spawn three healing items for Mr Game and Watch. However, the variants between one and nine are quite important. A one will damage Mr Game and Watch quite severely, leaving him open to KO's quicker. However, a 9 will instantly KO if it lands on a foe.
