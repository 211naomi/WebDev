Top 3 Places I've Been To Or Lived In

Dubai

The hotel was incredible, they had a pool on the 30th floor with a glass floor. The ocean was beutiful and the beaches were abselutley incredible. We got to see the tallest building in the world, and if I thought the collesium was big, bot was I wrong. It was so big it looked like a spaceship, I felt like a molecule standing next to it.

Zambia

When I was living in Zambia, the local people were so nice and the school was incredible. The students made me feel welcome and appreciated and during lunch we all played soccer. We also visited the victoria falls.

Italy

First of all, the food. The food was incredible, they had amazing pizza and pasta, especially the gelato. And not to forget the archetecture, the collesium was huge, I felt like an ant standing inside of the collesium.
