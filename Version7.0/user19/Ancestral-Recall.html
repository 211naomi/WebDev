Ancestral Recall is a blue instant, and one of the five boons printed in the original run of Magic. It is frequently cited as Blue's most powerful card, and it is one of the Power Nine. It is one of Mark Poole's most recognized pieces of art. Ancestral Recall was originally called "Ancestral Memories." It was originally going to be a common, but Richard Garfield was persuaded to make it a rare instead. This didn't stop Ancestral Recall from being unbalanced. The ability to draw three cards with no drawback allowed many decks to become dangerously fast. Eventually, Ancestral Recall was banned in all formats but Vintage, where it is restricted.
