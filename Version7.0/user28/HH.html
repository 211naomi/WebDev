The Headless Horseman

Now some would argue that the headless horseman is, in fact, a ghoul and NOT a cryptid. I disagree. To me the Headless Horseman is a fascinating creature of legend, his existence is unproven though many believe he is real! Therefore he has ranked highly at Number 2 on the Cryptid Top Five!

The headless horseman is believed to have been a soldier before his death, getting hit in the skull by a cannonball during battle, leaving him to roam his village, Sleepy Hollow, for eternity searching for his head. While this is only one reiteration of the story, some legends say he actually carries his disembodied head with him, it is largely accepted that after more than one hundred years of unrest he is most certainly a mythical being.

"Suddenly, his eye was caught by a light rising from the ground in the cemetery. He stopped, his heart pounding in fear. Before his startled eyes, a white mist burst forth from an unmarked grave and formed into a large horse carrying a headless rider".

An excerpt from the retelling of Washington Irving’s The Legend of Sleepy Hollow.
