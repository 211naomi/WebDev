Lena's Top 5 Favorite Cryptids

You may be wondering what exactly is a cryptid, we're so glad you asked!
A cryptid is known as an animal and/or humanoid creature whose existence cannot be substantiated. Sometimes even mythological legendary characters can be considered cryptids as well. A popular cryptid you may have already heard of is the illusive Yeti.

The yeti is a very well known cryptid who is incredibly similar to bigfoot. Both of these creatures have large followings, communities that believe in their existence, while also getting attention from many skeptics. The yeti originales from hymalayan folklore, known as very dangerous and monstrous creatures, the yeti is better known in western culture as the abominable snowman. There are many many cryptids floating around in history and hidden in the old lore of society, and chances are you’ve only heard of very few of them!

The NOT DEER!!



The "Not Deer!" is a very strange form of cryptid. They look exactly like regular deer, however, don't be fooled by this innocent appearance! They are nothing like the mammal they embody. Being in a not deer’s presence leaves you feeling uncomfortable and wrong overall, a gut reaction will tell you that something is not right in the picture before, giving an overwhelming sense of fight or flight. They are especially common in the Blue Ridge Mountains and Appalachia and often you will hear stories from hikers and deer hunters encountering deer that are just Wrong. So would you stick around? Or would a sense of self preservation take you away before you could investigate the eerie feeling? The strange nature of this phenomenon means it is incredibly hard to catch them on camera. But be warned! Gut insticts are usually right and these deer give you a feeling that is just plain Wrong!
