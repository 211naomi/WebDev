Number 1!

Mothman

Mothman is undeniably the most commonly discussed cryptid in the subcultural following. And has therefore been placed at

NUMBER ONE

on the Cryptid Top Five.

Mothman is one of the most well known cryptids around that doesn't belong to a species. He is an incredibly large humanoid creature with giant wings, though his most prominent feature is his piercing red eyes.

There is actually a very large mothman subculture! Particularly in Point Pleasant, West Virginia where he originates. There they have a Mothman stature, history museam, merchandise stores and more. It's a great tourist spot! Want to learn about visiting Point Pleasant? Check out this source
