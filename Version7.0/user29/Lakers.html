Intro About Minnesota Timberwolves
The Minnesota Timberwolves (also commonly known as the Wolves) are an American professional basketball team based in Minneapolis.

Minnesota Timberwolves

-Fun Facts

The Wolves made the playoffs eight consecutive times from 1997 to 2004.

NBA basketball returned to the Twin Cities in 1989 for the first time since the Minneapolis Lakers 1986 departed for Los Angeles in 1960.

-How the Timberwolves got their name ?

A most appropriate nickname was chosen for the state's NBA team, as Minnesota is home to the largest population of timberwolves in the lower 48 states. Here are some facts about the timberwolf: There are about 1,200 wolves in Minnesota, accounting for all but approximately 75 others in the continental U.S.
