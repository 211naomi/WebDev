 History 

 
 France, officially French Republic, 
             French France or République Française, country of northwestern Europe. 
             Historically and culturally among the most important nations in the Western 
             world, France has also played a highly significant role in international affairs, 
             with former colonies in every corner of the globe. Bounded by the Atlantic Ocean 
             and the Mediterranean Sea, the Alps and the Pyrenees, France has long provided a 
             geographic, economic, and linguistic bridge joining northern and southern Europe. 
             It is Europe’s most important agricultural producer and one of the world’s leading industrial powers.
