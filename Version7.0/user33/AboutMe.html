About Me

Want to know a little bit about me, eh?

Short Biography

I was born in 2003 in a town called Wyoming, Minnesota. About two years into my life I was diagnosed with Autism, so me and my family moved down to Arkansas to get help for me.
I believe going down south worked for me! I learned how to speak, interact with others and learn from mistakes.

I lived there for about four years until it was time to come back home.

My Interests

My interests include playing video games, drawing, and going on walks. I'm quite shy at first, but when we get to know each other a little more, I assure you we'll be the best of pals!
